1. Main Features

How to Change Partner Settings

  • You can change your partner from the Settings screen. Customize your companion that stays by your side and warmly watches over your daily records.

Home Screen

  • Heart Tree: Grows each time you post a diary entry. The more you continue, the richer it becomes, making your daily accumulation visible.
  • Post History: Check your number of posts and consecutive days at a glance. Achievement motivates you to continue.

Diary Entry

  • Normal Mode: Record daily events and feelings freely.
  • Dream Diary Mode: Dream analysis is enabled to analyze and comment on your dream world. Turn the blue line section ON for dream diary mode, OFF for normal mode.
  • AI Comments: Your partner gives gentle comments based on your content. Makes reflection enjoyable.

Diary List

  • Filtering (bookmarks/with images/dream diaries only, etc.) allows you to quickly narrow down the records you want to see.

Dashboard

  • Emotion Monitoring: Check daily positive/negative trends
  • Emotion Calendar: Intuitively grasp daily mood trends with colors
  • Radar Chart: Visualize emotional balance
  • Word Cloud: Display frequently used words at a glance
  • Weekly/Monthly Reports: Check AI-summarized trends (watch ads or subscribe)
  • Day-of-week Emotion Trends: Discover weekly rhythms with average scores by day
  • Monthly Emotion Trends: Understand changes in early, mid, and late month
  • Emotional Stability: Visualize heart fluctuations and check stability

2. How to Use the App

Recommended Usage

  • Even 1-3 lines daily is OK! The more you continue, the more your "Heart Tree" grows.
  • The Heart Tree might super-evolve when it breaks its limits...?
  • Think of diary writing as communication with your partner.
  • Writing events and emotions with specific words makes analysis and reflection more enjoyable.
  • For dream diaries, write immediately after waking up. Vivid content remains, improving dream analysis accuracy.

4. Tips for Continuing

  • Normal diary example: "Had lunch with a colleague today. The new pasta place was delicious and my mood went UP!"
  • Dream diary example: "Dream of running on the beach. The sky was pink and fantastical. The afterglow remained even after waking up."
  • Even short entries are OK. The more you continue, the more it becomes a habit, and you'll see your changes and growth.
  • Including emotional words and proper nouns doubles the fun when looking back later.

5. Troubleshooting

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Diary registration fails: Check your internet connection and try again after a few minutes.
  • Diary registration is slow: Partner comment registration may take 20-30 seconds. Please wait patiently.
  • Nothing displays on dashboard: Please check the dashboard after writing at least 3 diary entries.
  • AI comments don't appear: Network environment may be unstable. Try reopening the app.
  • Heart Tree doesn't grow: It grows when you post diaries. It may take some time for posts to be reflected.
